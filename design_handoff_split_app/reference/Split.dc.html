How to use
It's a real prototype
Create groups, log expenses and settle up — balances recompute live and persist between reloads. The phone to the left is fully interactive.
1
First use — tap Reset to empty below, then create your first group.
2
Log an expense — open Trip to Byron → Add expense → Save.
3
Settle up — on the Balances tab tap Settle and confirm.
